About the Founder

Brad Chun grew up in Honolulu, graduating from Maryknoll High School and then Seattle University, with a degree in Electrical Engineering. Following college, Brad’s interest in aviation led him to work on aircraft systems as an Engineer at United Airlines; then at the Boeing Commercial Aircraft Company. After more than three decades in the aircraft engineering industry, Brad’s last position prior to retirement brought him back to the islands at Hawaiian Airlines.

Brad has been doing home renovations, improvements, repairs and managing multiple rental properties over the past 20 years. He founded Akamai Residential Services upon retirement from his corporate job, after his parents shared horror stories of how unreliable, unprofessional and costly residential repairs have been for some of their friends and kapuna on fixed incomes. Brad started this business to bring back competent, reliable, professional and reasonable residential services to local residents.

Hawaii is a magnificent place to live and Akamai Residential Services wants to ensure that we help keep your home both beautiful and functional without costing a fortune to maintain, upgrade or renovate. To help keep our planet green and healthy, Akamai has partnered with the Nature Conservancy to have a tree planted with every completed project so that your home repair can have a lasting, positive impact.

From my Ohana to yours, have a safe and blessed year and I hope to have the opportunity to serve you in the near future!
